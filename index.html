I am a second year PhD student at MIT supported by an NSF GRFP Fellowship and advised by Alexander Postnikov. My main research area is enumerative and algebraic combinatorics, and Schubert polynomials/Schur polynomials, as well as pattern avoidance in permutations, are topics that particularly interest me. I completed my undergraduate degree at Harvard in mathematics.
